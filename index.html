MedPlus+

Connecting Patients to Doctors

MedPlus+ is a medical app that allows you to connect with experienced doctors for virtual consultations. Our platform provides convenient access to healthcare professionals from the comfort of your home.

With MedPlus+, you can discuss your health concerns, get medical advice, and receive prescriptions when necessary.

Location-Based Pricing

We understand that healthcare costs can vary based on your location and financial circumstances. Our algorithm calculates a small fee for each consultation, taking into account your location and ability to pay. This ensures that our services remain affordable and accessible to everyone.
